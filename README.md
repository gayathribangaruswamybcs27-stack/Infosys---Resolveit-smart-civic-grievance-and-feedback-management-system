**Problem Statement**
In many cities, citizens face difficulties in reporting civic issues such as potholes, water leakage, drainage problems, streetlight failures, and garbage disposal. The traditional complaint registration methods are slow, inefficient, and lack transparency.
ResolveIt aims to solve this problem by providing a digital platform that ensures:
Easy complaint submission
Real-time complaint tracking
Transparent grievance resolution
Faster response and accountability.
**Objectives**
To provide a user-friendly platform for complaint registration
To enable real-time complaint tracking
To ensure transparency in grievance handling
To reduce manual paperwork
To improve communication between citizens and authorities
